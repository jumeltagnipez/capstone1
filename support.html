Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the advantage of using a travel agency? Travel agencies have direct connections with the airlines. When availing of the services of a travel agency, you are given the option of booking an airline ticket first without having to have the ticket issued right away. Booking a ticket is equivalent to reserving your seat on your chosen flight option. When using a travel agency, we can book your airline ticket for you and hold it until the deadline given by the airline. You can opt to wait until the deadline before we issue the ticket. This gives you a chance to think about your option and decision without having to worry about booking and buying right away.

2. What’s the cheapest destination if I’m going on a package tour? The Asian package to Bangkok and Philippine local packages.

3. What are the countries that do not require visas for Filipinos? Brazil, Indonesia(except business trip), Thailand, Brunei, Malaysia, Cambodia, Peru, Palau(except for business trip), Singapore, Taiwan (provided with electronic visa which can be applied when holding a valid US, Canada, Japan, UK, Schengen, Australia, and New Zealand visa), Vietnam, Morocco, Mongolia, Israel.

4. When is the best time to travel to the United States of America? During off peak periods, from 01 September to 15 December and 01 February to 15 March.

5. What is the baggage allowance for Asia, Europe and the U.S.? Baggage allowance for Asia and Europe is 20 kilos and for the U.S. it is 2pcs, 50lbs/pc.

6. What are the requirements for first time passport applicants? A. Valid I.D. (driver’s license, SSS I.D., Company I.D., school I.D., PRC I.D., voter’s I.D.) B. Authenticated birth certificate C. Authenticated marriage contract (for married and minor applicants) D. Personal appearance E. Print-out of appointment confirmation F. Fully accomplished passport application form

7. How much are the airport terminal fees in the Philippines? International Php 550.00
